Clustering And QC Evidence

Optional section. Use this near the top only when clustering QC materially affects the interpretation, such as for high-mito clusters, tiny clusters, poor silhouette support, single-sample clusters, likely contaminants, or possible doublets.

Verdict

One concise but substantive paragraph that states the favored identity, notes the main alternative interpretations when relevant, and explains why the current conclusion fits best across all evidence lines together.

Marker Evidence

First paragraph: summarize the cell-level ranked markers and what lineage or state they imply.

Second paragraph: summarize the pseudobulk ranked markers and explain whether they reinforce or complicate the cell-level readout.

Specificity Evidence

Explain whether the proposed markers are concentrated in the target cluster or shared with neighboring states. Refer explicitly to dotplots, violins, UMAPs, and any relevant custom panels.

Pathway And Activity Evidence

One paragraph that explains how MSigDB, DoRothEA, and PROGENy support, refine, or fail to support the marker-based interpretation. Do not just list terms; explain what they add biologically.
