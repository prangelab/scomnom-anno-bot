Phase 5 DE Report

Contrast Definition
State the cluster, annotation layer, contrast key, and A-versus-B pair.
Explain the biological meaning of the contrast.

Verdict
Give one concise paragraph summarizing what the contrast appears to be doing in this cluster.
State whether the conclusion is pseudobulk-backed, cell-level-only, or discordant across sources.

Evidence Summary
Combine testability, DA context, gene-level DE, directionality, and source agreement into one tight section.
Say which side is higher, what kind of biological change that side seems to represent, and whether the evidence is concordant.
For sex-related contrasts, include the autosomal readout here as a compact second pass beyond the X or Y-linked hits.

Pathway, Activity, And Enrichment Evidence
Explain how DoRothEA, MSigDB, PROGENy, and any relevant enrichment outputs support or complicate the gene-level story.
Name the actual pathways, regulators, or condition-specific enrichment patterns that matter.

Validation Plots
Describe only the plots that actually clarified the memo.
Reuse existing volcanoes, dotplots, violins, heatmaps, and enrichment panels first, and explain what they show.

Interpretation
Give one short integrative paragraph explaining what this cluster appears to be doing biologically in the requested contrast and what kind of change it represents.

Caveats Or Next-Step Notes
Use this section only when the signal is weak, technically suspect, inflation-prone, or would benefit from targeted follow-up.
