Phase 4 DE Overview

Atlas-level contrast triage template

Scope

State the active context, annotation layer, DE round, contrast key, and selected contrast. Explain the biological meaning of the contrast and fold in only the run settings that materially affect interpretation, such as whether pseudobulk exists, whether cell-level DE is the only source, and any special restrictions or thresholds.

Atlas-Level Signal

Give the broad atlas-level readout in one tight paragraph. Say whether the contrast is broad, focal, or weak; which side is more coherent when the contrast is directional; and what kind of biological change that direction seems to represent. Interpret the figures directly rather than calling them strong in generic terms.

Key Drivers And Limits

Combine the strongest responding clusters, any meaningful DA context, and the main limitations into one compact memo section. Say which clusters really drive the biology, which side is higher in those clusters, what that side appears to be doing, and whether the story is mainly transcriptional, compositional, or both.

Cluster Why It Matters Higher Side Biological Reading
C00 Main driver, weak lead, or skipped cluster. State the biological side explicitly. One short sentence on what kind of change it represents.

Pathway, Regulator, And Enrichment Evidence

Summarize only the pathway or regulator evidence that materially sharpens the interpretation. Name concrete pathways, regulators, or enrichment patterns and explain whether they agree with the gene-level story. If condition-specific enrichment exists, state what it adds beyond standard cluster enrichment and DE-derived enrichment.

Priority Shortlist For Phase 5

Name only the clusters that truly deserve a phase 5 deep dive and explain briefly why each one is worth the extra effort.

Practical Takeaway

End with a short paragraph stating whether the contrast produced a useful atlas-level biological story, which side is most interpretable if the contrast is directional, and where the most trustworthy signal sits.
