Phase 1 Overview

Template for a first-pass overview report.

Scope

Summarize what object or subset is being reviewed, what clustering round is the backbone, and what the purpose of the first pass is.

Global Evidence

Give a high-level summary of the final UMAP and the all-cluster heatmaps. Explain what major compartments or lineages are visible.

Global QC Context

Summarize the overall QC state of the dataset or subset. Mention load-and-filter QC if relevant for phase 1, and clustering QC where it materially affects interpretation.

Phase 1 Decision Summary

  • List clusters that are already final-ready.
  • List clusters that should be subsetted or deep-dived next.
  • List clusters that should be treated cautiously because of QC or contamination concerns.

End with a short practical takeaway that says where the real remaining work is.
