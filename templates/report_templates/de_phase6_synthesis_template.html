Available Layers And Evidence

List which phase 4 and phase 5 reports were used, which DE evidence sources are available, and whether pseudobulk support exists anywhere in the synthesis.

Cross-Layer DE Summary

Give the short atlas-level story for the contrast and say whether the signal is broad, focal, weak, compartment-specific, or mixed.

Compartment-Level Themes

Summarize the main biological themes by broad compartment. Prefer a small number of interpretable themes over exhaustive cluster-by-cluster restatement.

Layer-Resolved Drivers

Explain which fine-grained populations are driving the broad signals and which broad effects sharpen, disappear, or change after refinement.

Process Panels

Describe the targeted custom process panels that were generated, why those genes were chosen, and what they show about the inferred biological process.

Integrated Interpretation

Turn the broad-to-fine signal into one coherent biological interpretation for the contrast.

Confidence And Caveats

State whether the synthesis is pseudobulk-backed, cell-level-only, concordant across layers, or still provisional. Be explicit about inflation risk, sparse subclusters, mixed programs, or technical ambiguity when relevant.

Practical Takeaway

End with a short paragraph stating the final biological message for the contrast and which populations matter most.
